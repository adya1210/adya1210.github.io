Career Highlights

Justin Bieber’s music career began in 2008 when he was discovered on YouTube by Scooter Braun. His debut single, “One Time,” quickly gained popularity, followed by the album “My World” which catapulted him into the limelight. Over the years, Justin has released multiple hit albums, including “Believe,” “Purpose,” and “Justice.” He has won numerous awards including Grammys, American Music Awards, and Billboard Music Awards. Beyond music, Justin has made appearances in films and collaborated with top artists globally. His journey has not only been about fame but also personal growth, facing challenges publicly, and using his platform to advocate for causes he cares about. Fans worldwide admire his dedication, talent, and evolution as an artist.
